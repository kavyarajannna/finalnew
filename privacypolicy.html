Privacy Notice

This privacy notice was last reviewed or revised on January 29, 2024.

At [Your Company Name], and for all applications ("Apps") we develop, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring compliance with data protection regulations. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard information for:

  • Our Company: Operations, services, and communication with clients and users.
  • Applications Developed for Customers: Including apps published on platforms like Google Play, App Store, Oculus Store, and Steam Store.

By using our services or applications, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Scope of the Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • Company Operations: Covers client interactions, business communications, marketing, and support services.
  • Developed Applications: Includes custom-built apps for clients, and any apps we publish or manage directly on their behalf.

For apps developed for our customers, we follow their specific privacy requirements and guidelines. Users should also review the privacy policies provided by our customers.

2. Information We Collect

We collect data necessary to provide and improve our services and apps effectively.

2.1 Company Operations

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and company details.
  • Project Information: Details of your requirements, feedback, and related communications.
  • Payment Information: Billing details, securely processed via trusted payment gateways.
  • Usage Data: Interaction with our website, emails, or other digital platforms.

2.2 Applications Developed

  • Account Information: Name, email, username, and profile details.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, IP address, and unique identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Interaction with app features, session details, and preferences.
  • Location Data: If required by app functionality, approximate or precise location.
  • Payment Information: Payment details for in-app purchases or subscriptions, processed by platform-specific payment systems (e.g., Google Play, App Store).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

3.1 For Company Operations

  • Deliver Services: Understand client needs and deliver customized solutions.
  • Customer Support: Address inquiries and resolve issues.
  • Analytics: Improve our services through insights from usage patterns.
  • Marketing and Communication: Share updates, newsletters, and promotional content (with consent).

3.2 For Developed Applications

  • App Functionality: Enable core features like user authentication, in-app purchases, or gameplay.
  • Performance Optimization: Enhance app performance and usability.
  • Personalization: Customize user experiences and content.
  • Compliance: Meet platform and legal requirements, including parental consent for apps used by children.

4. Sharing Your Information

We prioritize data privacy and transparency. Information may be shared in the following cases:

4.1 For Company Services

  • With Service Providers: Trusted vendors for cloud storage, payment processing, or marketing.
  • Compliance: When required by law or in response to legal requests.

4.2 For Applications Developed

  • With Platform Providers: To enable platform-specific integrations (e.g., Google Play Games, Steam Achievements).
  • Customer-Specific Requirements: Data collection and sharing based on client-defined policies and practices.
  • Third-Party Tools: Integration with analytics, advertising, or other APIs used within the app.

5. Data Security

We implement stringent security measures to protect the information we handle, ensuring the safety of both company operations and data from the apps we develop.

5.1 Security Practices

  • Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted during storage and transmission (e.g., SSL/TLS).
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel have access to systems and data.
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security reviews to identify and address vulnerabilities.
  • Third-Party Compliance: Service providers we use comply with recognized security standards.

5.2 Specific to Apps

  • Secure Integration: All apps developed include secure APIs and comply with platform security requirements (e.g., Google Play, App Store, Oculus Store, Steam).
  • Data Minimization: Apps are designed to collect only the data necessary for their intended functionality.

5.3 Limitations

While we strive for maximum security, no system is entirely foolproof. In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected parties promptly and take corrective actions.

6. Cookies and Tracking

6.1 Use of Cookies

Cookies and similar technologies may be used for:

  • Session Management: To remember user sessions and preferences.
  • Analytics: To analyze website and app performance.
  • Personalization: To deliver customized content or ads (if applicable).

6.2 Tracking Technologies in Apps

For apps, we may use:

  • Device Identifiers: To optimize performance or track session activities.
  • Analytics Tools: Integration with services like Firebase, Google Analytics, or similar platforms to improve user experience.

6.3 Managing Cookies and Tracking

Users can:

  • Adjust browser or device settings to block or manage cookies.
  • Opt out of tracking through in-app settings or platform preferences (e.g., App Tracking Transparency on iOS).

7. Children’s Privacy

7.1 General Policy

Apps developed for or used by children comply with laws like COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act) and platform-specific policies (e.g., Google Play’s Families Policy).

7.2 Data Collection for Children

For child-directed apps:

  • Data collection is limited to what is necessary for the app’s functionality.
  • Personal information is not collected without verifiable parental consent.

7.3 Compliance in Customer Apps

When developing apps for clients targeting children, we ensure compliance with:

  • Google Play Families Policy: For family-friendly content and features.
  • App Store Guidelines: Specifically for child-directed apps and data handling.
  • Oculus Store and Steam: Age-appropriate content and restricted data collection.

7.4 Reporting Concerns

If you believe a child’s data has been collected without consent, contact us at privacy@[yourdomain].com, and we will take immediate action.

8. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to fulfill its intended purpose or comply with legal obligations.

8.1 For Company Operations

  • Client Data: Retained for the duration of the business relationship and securely archived after project completion, unless otherwise requested.
  • Financial Records: Retained as required by tax and accounting laws.
  • Communication Data: Emails and support requests may be stored for quality assurance and troubleshooting purposes.

8.2 For Developed Applications

  • User Data: Retention policies vary based on the app’s functionality and platform guidelines. Data is retained only as long as needed for core features, such as saving user progress or preferences.
  • Users may request deletion of their data via in-app settings or by contacting the app’s support team.
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained for research and optimization.

8.3 Data Deletion

For apps we develop for customers, data deletion policies align with client requirements and platform compliance (e.g., GDPR, CCPA). Users can request data deletion through the app’s settings or by contacting support.

9. Store Publishing Compliance

We ensure that all apps published on platforms such as Google Play, App Store, Oculus Store, and Steam Store comply with their specific data privacy and security requirements.

9.1 Google Play

  • Compliance with Google’s User Data Policy, including transparency about data usage through the Data Safety section.
  • Adherence to the Families Policy for apps targeting children, ensuring parental consent and age-appropriate content.

9.2 App Store

  • Full disclosure via Privacy Nutrition Labels, detailing data collected, its usage, and whether it’s linked to individual users.
  • Compliance with Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework for user consent on tracking activities.

9.3 Oculus Store

  • Data collected from VR devices (e.g., tracking, spatial data) is handled per Oculus’s privacy guidelines.
  • Apps ensure minimal data collection and provide clear communication of their purposes to users.

9.4 Steam Store

  • Compliance with Valve’s privacy requirements for multiplayer games, in-game transactions, and community interactions.
  • Clear disclosure of data use, including achievements, leaderboards, and other features.

9.5 Platform-Specific Opt-Outs

Users can adjust settings to disable tracking or limit data collection through platform features (e.g., Google Account preferences, iOS settings, or Steam privacy controls).

10. User Rights

We respect users' rights to control their personal data, whether collected through our company operations or apps we develop.

10.1 Rights for Company Interactions

  • Access: Users may request a copy of the data we hold about them.
  • Correction: Users can request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Users may request data deletion, subject to legal and contractual obligations.
  • Data Portability: Provide user data in a structured, commonly used format upon request.

10.2 Rights for Developed Apps

For apps, users can exercise their rights directly within the app or by contacting the customer or support team. Rights align with regulations like GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act).

10.3 How to Exercise Rights

Users can contact us at privacy@[yourdomain].com to submit a request. Requests will be addressed within the timelines mandated by applicable laws.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or new features in our services and applications.

11.1 Notification of Changes

  • Updates will be posted on our website and, where applicable, within the apps we develop.
  • For significant changes, we will notify users via email or in-app notifications.

11.2 Effective Date of Changes

All updates will include an “Effective Date” at the top of this Privacy Policy. Continued use of our services or apps after updates constitutes your acceptance of the revised terms.
